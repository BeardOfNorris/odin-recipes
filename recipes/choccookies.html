Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate lover’s dream with these rich, fudgy Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies. Each bite delivers a deep, cocoa-infused decadence, perfectly balanced by generous pockets of melty, semi-sweet chocolate chips. These cookies boast a soft, chewy center with slightly crisp edges, offering a delightful texture that practically melts in your mouth. Whether you’re dunking them in a cold glass of milk or savoring them straight from the oven, these double-chocolate treats are a heavenly escape into pure bliss—irresistible, comforting, and oh-so-satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup cocoa powder
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into butter mixture until well blended.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven just until set, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the cookie sheets before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  6. Enjoy!
